Every Type of Business Funding, in One Place

Not sure what type of funding fits your situation? We'll match you to the right lender automatically — here's what our network offers.

Merchant Cash Advance

Get a lump sum today and repay it as a small percentage of your daily sales. No fixed monthly payment — you pay more when business is good, less when it's slow.

$5K – $20M+

Business Term Loan

A fixed amount deposited upfront, repaid over a set schedule with predictable payments. Ideal if you need capital for a specific goal — expansion, hiring, or inventory.

$10K – $500K

SBA Loans

Government-backed loans with lower rates and longer repayment terms — best for businesses that qualify and want the most affordable long-term financing. Longer process — best for established businesses with strong documentation.

$50K – $5M

Equipment Financing

Need a truck, commercial kitchen, or new machinery? Finance the equipment directly — the asset itself acts as collateral, making approval easier than a standard loan.

Up to $500K

Business Line of Credit

A revolving credit line you draw from only when you need it — and only pay interest on what you actually use. Perfect for managing cash flow gaps between invoices.
