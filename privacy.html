Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 12, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 12, 2026

1. About TrustLine

TrustLine Financial, LLC ("TrustLine," "we," "us," or "our") operates trustlinebiz.com as a lead generation and marketing service. We are not a direct lender. By submitting your information through our site, you are requesting to be matched with third-party lending partners who may offer business funding or payment processing products.

2. Information We Collect

When you complete our quote form, we collect:

  • Full name and business name
  • Phone number and email address
  • Monthly revenue range
  • Device and browser information (IP address, browser type, referring URL) collected automatically via cookies and server logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Match you with lending partners and financial service providers who may meet your needs
  • Contact you via phone, email, or SMS about funding offers (see Section 5 — TCPA)
  • Improve our website and marketing performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing Your Information

We share your information with:

  • Lending partners and affiliates — third-party companies that may provide funding or payment processing products to you
  • Service providers — vendors who help us operate our platform (e.g., hosting, analytics, email delivery)
  • Legal authorities — when required by law, subpoena, or to protect our rights

We do not sell your personal information to unaffiliated data brokers or list vendors for their own marketing purposes.

5. TCPA Consent & Communications

By submitting the quote form on our site, you expressly consent to receive communications from TrustLine and its lending partners via phone calls, emails, and SMS/text messages — including calls and texts made using an automatic telephone dialing system (ATDS) or pre-recorded/artificial voice — at the phone number and email address you provide. You are not required to consent as a condition of purchasing any goods or services. Standard message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text, or contacting us at the information below.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze site traffic and improve user experience. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may not function correctly as a result.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL/TLS encryption, to protect your information in transit. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. To submit a request, contact us at the address below. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

9. Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Your continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
