# TrustLine — Creative Brief (Meta Ads)
**Target:** Florida small business owners seeking fast funding
**Budget stage:** Seed ($150–300/month)
**Required creative:** 3 static images minimum, 1 video optional

---

## Creative Set 1 — Speed Angle (P1)

**Concept:** The headline IS the ad. Big number. Gold on navy. Done.

**Visual:**
- Background: Deep navy (`#081426`) gradient
- Large gold text: "Funded in 24 Hours"
- Subtext (white, smaller): "100+ private lenders · No bank required"
- Bottom: TrustLine logo + "trustlinebiz.com"
- Optional: Speed visual — lightning bolt, timer icon, or motion blur lines (abstract, not stock photo)

**Copy:**
```
Primary text (125 chars max):
Florida business owners — stop waiting on banks.
Get $5,000–$500,000 in working capital in as little as 24 hours.
Free to check. No credit impact.

Headline: Get Funded in 24 Hours
Description: 100+ lenders · Free to apply · All FL businesses welcome
CTA: Learn More
```

**Sizes to produce:**
- 1080×1080 (Feed)
- 1080×1350 (Portrait Feed — typically higher CTR)
- 1080×1920 (Stories/Reels — crop key content to center 60%)

## Optional: Video Creative (15 seconds) — P2

**Only produce if capacity exists. Static images first.**

**Script (15s):**
```
[0–2s] TEXT ON SCREEN: "Florida business owners..."
[2–5s] TEXT ON SCREEN: "Banks turned you down?" / "Funding taking months?"
[5–10s] TEXT ON SCREEN: "TrustLine matches you to 100+ private lenders"
         VISUAL: Fast-moving text/number counter — $5K → $50K → $500K
[10–13s] TEXT ON SCREEN: "Approved in 24 hours. Money in 48."
[13–15s] LOGO + CTA: "trustlinebiz.com — Free to apply"
```

**Voiceover (optional):** Direct, conversational male or female voice. No music that feels salesy or celebratory. Confident, matter-of-fact tone.

**No face needed.** Text-on-screen with motion typography performs well for financial products and requires no filming.

---

## Compliance Checklist for All Creatives

- [ ] No language: "guaranteed approval", "you will be approved", "no credit check" (we do a soft check)
- [ ] No specific interest rates or APR in ad (TrustLine is a marketplace, not a lender)
- [ ] No imagery of cash stacks, luxury cars, mansions, extreme wealth
- [ ] No bank logos (trademark issues)
- [ ] TCPA consent language in Instant Form (already configured in form setup)
- [ ] "Free to apply" and "no credit impact" are factually accurate — keep these
- [ ] Testimonials are real and attributable — Marcus T., David K., Sandra R. from homepage
