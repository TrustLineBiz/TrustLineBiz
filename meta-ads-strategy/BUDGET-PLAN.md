# TrustLine — Meta Budget Plan
**Range:** $150–$300/month
**Platform:** Meta only (Facebook + Instagram)

---

## Why One Platform at This Budget

The **70/20/10 framework** is designed for budgets where multiple platforms are viable. At $150–300/month in financial services, splitting across platforms means:
- Google Search: minimum viable is ~$600–800/month for financial keywords in FL
- LinkedIn: minimum viable is ~$1,500/month (B2B, high CPL)
- TikTok: viable at $300+ but requires video creative

**Decision: 100% Meta until monthly budget reaches $800+.** Concentrate to learn faster.

## Budget Scaling Triggers

Scale budget **only when** one of these is true:
- CPL is consistently below $40 for 2+ weeks
- You have 1+ confirmed funded deal from Meta leads
- Instant Form → application rate exceeds 30%

**Scale rule:** Increase by 20% maximum per week. Do not double budget overnight — it resets the Meta learning phase.

| Budget milestone | When to reach it | What to unlock |
|-----------------|-----------------|----------------|
| $150/mo | Now | Seed testing, 1 campaign |
| $300/mo | Month 2 (if CPL < $50) | Add retargeting campaign |
| $600/mo | Month 3–4 (proven CPL) | Add 2nd audience/interest set |
| $1,000/mo | Month 5–6 | Consider adding Google Search |
| $2,000+/mo | Month 7+ | Full multi-platform (Google + Meta + TikTok) |

## Kill Rules (3× Kill Rule)

Pause any ad that has spent **3× your target CPL** with zero conversions.

| Target CPL | Kill threshold | Example |
|-----------|----------------|---------|
| $30 | Spent $90 with 0 leads | Pause |
| $50 | Spent $150 with 0 leads | Pause |

Apply this at the ad level — not the ad set level. If 2 of 3 ads are killed, the surviving ad gets all the budget. That's fine — let it run.
