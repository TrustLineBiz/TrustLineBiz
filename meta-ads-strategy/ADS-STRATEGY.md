## Executive Summary

TrustLine is a **lead generation service** for business funding — matching small business owners to a network of 100+ private lenders. The goal of Meta ads is to generate qualified form submissions (name, business, phone, email, revenue) at the lowest possible cost per lead.

**Critical compliance note:** Because TrustLine facilitates access to credit products (loans, MCAs, lines of credit), all Meta campaigns **must be declared under Special Ad Category: Credit**. This restricts targeting options but is non-negotiable — running without it risks account suspension.

### What $150–300/month realistically delivers on Meta

| Budget | Est. Daily Spend | Est. Monthly Impressions | Est. Monthly Leads |
|--------|-----------------|--------------------------|-------------------|
| $150/mo | $5/day | 3,000–5,000 | 3–8 leads |
| $300/mo | $10/day | 6,000–10,000 | 6–15 leads |

> Financial services CPM on Meta averages $30–50. At these budgets, you are in **seed testing mode** — learning which creative and audience combinations work before scaling. Every lead at this stage is data. Target CPL: $20–50 (Instant Form) or $40–100 (website click).

## Special Ad Category: Credit — What This Means

Declare on every campaign. This changes targeting as follows:

| Normal Targeting | Special Ad Category (Credit) |
|-----------------|------------------------------|
| Age targeting | ❌ Not allowed |
| Gender targeting | ❌ Not allowed |
| ZIP code exclusions | ❌ Not allowed |
| Lookalike audiences | ❌ Replaced by Special Ad Audiences |
| Interest targeting | ✅ Allowed (business interests) |
| Geographic (state/city) | ✅ Allowed (min. 15-mile radius) |
| Behavioral targeting | ✅ Limited set allowed |
| Retargeting (website visitors) | ✅ Allowed |

**Special Ad Audiences:** Once you have 100+ leads, create a Special Ad Audience based on converters. This is the closest equivalent to a lookalike under credit restrictions — Meta finds people similar to your converters without using restricted attributes.

## Creative Strategy

### Ad Format Priority (Meta, $150–300 budget)

| Priority | Format | Why | Quantity |
|----------|--------|-----|----------|
| P1 | Static image (1080×1080 + 1080×1350) | Fastest to produce, consistent delivery | 3 variants |
| P2 | Static image with headline text overlay | Gold on navy — on-brand, thumb-stopping | 2 variants |
| P3 | Short video (15s) | Highest CTR when available | 1 if capacity exists |

**Start with 2–3 static images only.** At $5–10/day, video may not generate enough impressions to learn. Get data from statics first.

### Creative Specifications (Meta)

| Format | Size | Text limit | Safe zone |
|--------|------|-----------|-----------|
| Feed image | 1080×1080px | Primary text: 125 chars | Keep key content central |
| Story / Reels | 1080×1920px | 250 chars | Bottom 250px = UI overlay |
| Feed portrait | 1080×1350px | 125 chars | 4:5 ratio |

**Brand application:**
- Background: Navy dark (`#081426`) or gradient navy
- Headline text: Gold (`#c9a84c`) — large, bold, 80–900 weight
- Body text: White on dark, or navy on white for light variants
- No bank imagery, no cash stacks, no luxury lifestyle
