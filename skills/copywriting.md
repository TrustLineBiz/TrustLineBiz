# Copywriting Skill — TrustLineBiz.com

## Brand Voice
- **Tone:** Direct, confident, modern — not corporate, not salesy
- **Audience:** Small business owners (non-technical, time-pressured)
- **Core promise:** Fast funding without bank bureaucracy
- **Key differentiators:** Speed (24–48h), volume (100+ lenders), ease (2-min form)

## Rules — Always Follow
- Never say "helping businesses grow" — too vague
- Never say "we cut through the red tape" — cliché
- Always include a number where possible (24 hours, $5K, 100+ lenders)
- CTAs must describe the action + outcome, not just the action
  - Good: "Check My Funding Options →"
  - Bad:  "Submit" / "Learn More" / "Get Started"
- Headlines: outcome first, mechanism second
  - Good: "Get $5,000–$500,000 in Business Funding in as Little as 24 Hours"
  - Bad:  "Fast and Easy Business Funding Solutions"

## Section-Specific Guidelines

### Hero Headline
Formula: [Specific outcome] + [Speed/ease] + [Who it's for]
Max 12 words. Must pass the "5-second test" — instantly clear.

### Sub-headline
1–2 sentences. Answers: How does it work? What makes it different?
Include: 100+ lenders, not a bank, no credit impact.

### CTA Buttons
- Primary: "Check My Funding Options →"
- Nav CTA: "Check My Options →"
- Final CTA section: "Check My Funding Options →"
- Form button: "Check My Funding Options →"
Do NOT change CTAs without strong A/B test reason.

### Testimonials
- Must sound like a real person talking — no corporate phrasing
- Must mention a specific dollar amount and outcome
- Must address a real objection (bank rejection, tight timeline, imperfect credit)
- Keep under 60 words each

### Trust Badges / Micro-copy
Always include these four near any CTA:
1. No credit score impact
2. No obligation
3. Results in minutes
4. 256-bit SSL encrypted / info never sold

## Placeholder Stats (Replace Before Launch)
The following are placeholder numbers. Replace with real data:
- "3,200+ Businesses Funded"
- "$180M+ Total Capital Deployed"
- "91% Approval Rate"
- "24 hrs Avg. Approval Time"

## Forbidden Phrases
- "helping businesses grow" (vague)
- "cutting through red tape" (cliché)
- "seamless experience" (meaningless)
- "innovative solution" (meaningless)
- "we're here for you" (empty)
- "taking your business to the next level" (cliché)
- any phrase that could apply to any financial company
